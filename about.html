Innovative Construction Methods

Domerius Services employs state-of-the-art building techniques that redefine efficiency, cost-effectiveness, and durability. Our approach includes:

  • Raft Foundation: Monolothical wall panel construction method fused with a breathing Geoplast foundation blocks ensuring twice the strength of conventional construction.
  • Structural Plastering: Reinforced with High strength galvanized Mesh, with a high 14Mpa plaster mix, ensuring resilience and longevity.
  • Alternative Building Slabs: Advanced polystyrene slabs from TASS Systems and Ikhaya Future House for superior insulation and reduced energy consumption.

By integrating these innovative materials, we deliver homes that are 5-20% more energy-efficient, significantly reducing heating and cooling costs. Our construction method eliminates the need for excessive labor, empowering a diverse workforce, including women in construction.
