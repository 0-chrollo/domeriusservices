Company Overview

Domerius Services is a leading architectural design and construction company that has been providing exceptional services since its establishment in 2016. Based in Gauteng, South Africa, Domerius Services specializes in high-quality residential and commercial architectural designs, sustainable construction, and project management.

With a dedicated team of professionals, the company is committed to delivering innovative and modern solutions tailored to clients' needs. Domerius Services ensures that every project meets the highest industry standards while maintaining a focus on affordability and sustainability.

Our Services

We offer a comprehensive range of services in the architectural and construction industry, including:

  • Residential Architecture – Custom home designs, renovations, and modern housing solutions.
  • Commercial Architecture – Office buildings, malls, and retail space planning.
  • Interior Architecture – Stylish and functional interior designs for homes and offices.
  • Sustainable Architecture – Eco-friendly and energy-efficient design solutions.
  • Historic Preservation – Restoration and conservation of heritage buildings.
  • Project Management – Full-scale management of construction projects from start to finish.
  • Social Housing Development – Affordable housing solutions for low- and middle-income families.
  • Construction Monitoring – Quality control and project supervision.
